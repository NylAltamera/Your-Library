Fate Breaker

Author: Victoria Aveyard

Description: The explosive finale to the Realm Breaker trilogy, where Corayne and her allies must defy impossible odds to stop a world-destroying enemy and decide the fate of all realms.

Kindling

Author: Traci Chee

Description: A powerful tale of rebellion and resilience, following five war-orphaned teens who are recruited to fight in a brutal war—only to discover that survival means facing both monsters and their own inner battles.

Darker by Four

Author: June CL Tan

Description: A lush urban fantasy steeped in Asian mythology, where a girl bound by a deadly contract and a boy haunted by demons must confront gods, curses, and their own intertwined destinies.

Extasia

Author: Claire Legrand

Description: A chilling feminist horror-fantasy set in a puritanical town, where a girl joins a coven of witches to harness dark powers against the monsters—both human and supernatural—that haunt her world.

Blood Scion

Author: Deborah Falaye

Description: Inspired by Yoruba mythology, this gripping fantasy follows Sloane, a girl with forbidden magic who is forced to fight for the very army that oppresses her people, torn between survival and resistance.

A Thousand Steps into Night

Author: Traci Chee

Description: A Japanese-inspired fantasy adventure where a girl is cursed to slowly turn into a demon, embarking on a perilous journey filled with trickster gods, monsters, and choices that test her humanity

Graceling

Author: Kristin Cashore

Description: A modern fantasy classic, introducing Katsa, a young woman born with a deadly Grace—an extraordinary skill for killing—who discovers her true strength lies not in violence but in challenging tyranny.

Terciel & Elinor

Author: Garth Nix

Description: A prequel to the beloved Old Kingdom series, telling the story of necromancer Terciel and courageous Elinor as they uncover dark secrets and face the rising threat of Death itself.
