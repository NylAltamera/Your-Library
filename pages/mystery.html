Mystery and Thriller

Solve thrilling mysteries and uncover hidden secrets!

If He Hollers Let Him Go

Author: Chester Himes

Description: A gripping psychological tale set in wartime Los Angeles, following a Black shipyard worker whose life unravels under the weight of racism, paranoia, and fear, blurring the line between reality and nightmare

Double Indemnity

Author: James Cain

Description: A dark, suspenseful story of lust and greed, where an insurance salesman is lured into a deadly scheme by a femme fatale, leading to betrayal, deception, and inevitable tragedy.

The Daughter of Time

Author: Josephine Tey

Description: An injured detective investigates from his hospital bed, unraveling the centuries-old mystery of King Richard III and questioning whether history’s judgment of him as a villain was ever true.

In A Lonely Place

Author: Dorothy Hughes

Description: A chilling noir novel that delves into the mind of a serial killer in postwar Los Angeles, where charm hides brutality and the city’s glittering façade masks deadly dark secrets.

Beat Not The Bones

Author: Charlotte Jay

Description: Set in New Guinea, this atmospheric mystery follows a young widow who refuses to accept her husband’s suspicious death, uncovering corruption, colonial tensions, and buried truths.

Casino Royale

Author: Lan Fleming

Description: The debut of James Bond, where the suave British spy takes on a high-stakes poker game against a dangerous Soviet agent, blending espionage, betrayal, and deadly glamour.

Crime and Punishment

Author: Fyodor Dostoevsky

Description: A philosophical masterpiece that follows Raskolnikov, a young student in St. Petersburg, who commits murder and is consumed by guilt, morality, and the search for redemption.

The Leaven-Worth Case

Author: Anna Katharine Greens

Description: One of the earliest American detective novels, featuring a wealthy man’s murder and a cunning investigation that exposes family rivalries, dark secrets, and betrayal.
